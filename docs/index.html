About Us

Founded in 2003 as a private Company offering Heavy Equipment and Machinery support services to the Industries in Kuwait, DIC has grown into a multi‑diversified contractor across Integrated Energy & Infrastructure sectors.

With 800+ human assets and 1000+ equipment assets, we are committed to becoming the market leader and preferred partner for our clients.
