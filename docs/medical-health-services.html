Medical & Health

Healthcare systems around the world have been facing unprecedented challenges in the recent days, hence the health service providers and suppliers are re-thinking the way to operate with innovative solutions. Globally Governments are keen on developing the public healthcare system in order to keep up with the rising demand, reflected by the ongoing expansionary healthcare projects. Also the major Energy segment clients have committed themselves in establishing a safe and healthy environment for their employees, contractors and all stakeholders.

DIC with its professional background in the Healthcare industry and with industry knowledge, believes in providing holistic services that fit the complexities of an ever-changing environment and offer innovative solutions to overcome them efficiently and sophistically.

DIC is committed to leveraging its expertise and resources to support the enhancement of public health systems and the promotion of occupational safety. With a focus on health science, risk assessment, and occupational health, DIC aims to ensure comprehensive care and regulatory compliance for its clients in the energy sector and beyond.

Safeguarding Well-being Across Diverse Sectors:

  • Health Science and Risk Assessment
  • Occupational Health and Industrial Hygiene
  • Representation of Medical Devices & Service Providers
  • Work Exposure Evaluation and Testing
  • Medical Tourism Support
