    Your Guide to DIC’s Sustainable Construction and Maintenance Services

    What makes DIC a preferred partner for sustainable construction?

    DIC is committed to sustainable construction by prioritizing shared innovation, health, and safety. We work closely with clients to transform living environments, infrastructure, and mobility with eco-friendly practices and advanced technologies.

    What services are included under Earth Moving and Civil Works?

    Our Earth Moving and Civil Works services encompass excavation, grading, soil stabilization, foundation preparation, and the construction of essential infrastructure such as roads, bridges, and drainage systems.

    What is the difference between Green and Brown Field Development?

    Green Field Development involves constructing new facilities on previously undeveloped land, while Brown Field Development focuses on redeveloping or repurposing existing sites, often addressing environmental remediation and upgrading infrastructure.

    What solutions does DIC provide for Waterproofing?

    DIC’s Waterproofing Solutions include advanced techniques and materials to protect buildings and structures from water infiltration, ensuring durability and preventing damage to both new constructions and existing structures.

    We strive to be the market leader through our core values of innovation, sustainability, and ethical practices, ensuring quality, health, safety, and environmental stewardship in all operations.
