ABOUT US

Al Dali International Co.
for General Trading & Contracting

Rising to Excellence

Leading Innovation & Growth

Founded in 2003 as a private Company offering Heavy Equipment and Machinery support services to the Industries in Kuwait, today DIC has exponentially grown and established itself as one of the most cherished multi diversified contractor across the State of Kuwait.

DIC's exponential growth since its modest beginning is a reflection of its commitment to customers, who come year after year. With dedicated and experienced human assets of 800+ employees and equipment assets exceeding 1,000+ nos. of Heavy Equipment & Machinery along with innovative technological partners, DIC is marching towards achieving its vision of becoming a market leader and preferred partner for Integrated Energy and Infrastructure sectors.

Striving for Excellence in Management

Quality, Health, Safety & Environment

DIC's commitment to Quality, Health, Safety, and Environmental stewardship is ingrained in its entire operations. It's visible in its core values, the work it performs, and in its people.

DIC believes that an essential factor in its business growth is the emphasis it places on Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment (QHSE). QHSE is a fundamental element in all its business activities and an integral part of service delivery workflows from all levels of employees and contractors.

DIC takes to heart the well-being of its employees. Its Company-wide emphasis on safety, extensive training programs, and quality-of-life benefits — all these are ways it maintains and develops the one asset that makes every success possible: the DIC team.
