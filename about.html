Rising to Excellence: DIC's Remarkable Journey

Leading Innovation & Growth

Founded in 2003 as a private Company offering Heavy Equipment and Machinery support services to the Industries in Kuwait, today DIC has exponentially grown and established itself as one of the most cherished multi diversified contractor across the State of Kuwait. DIC's exponential growth since its modest beginning is a reflection of its commitment to customers, who come year after year.

DIC with dedicated and experienced Human assets of 800+ nos. and with Equipment assets exceeding 1000+ nos. of Heavy Equipment & Machinery along with Innovative technological partners, is marching towards achieving its vision of becoming a market leader and preferred partner for Integrated Energy and Infrastructure sectors services provider closer to the heart of its clients.

Striving for Excellence in Management

Quality, Health, Safety & Environment

DIC's commitment to Quality, Health, Safety, and Environmental stewardship are ingrained in its entire operations. It's visible in its core values, the work it performs, and in its people.

DIC believes that an essential factor in its business growth is the emphasis it places on Quality, Health, Safety, and Environment (QHSE). QHSE is a fundamental element in all its business activities and an integral part of service delivery workflows from all levels of employees and contractors.

DIC complies with the most stringent of the legal, international, national, customer, and other applicable safety and ecological standards and requirements in all its business operations.

DIC takes to heart the well-being of its employees. Its Company-wide emphasis on safety, extensive training programs, quality-of-life, and benefits – all these are ways it maintains and develops the one asset that makes every success possible: the DIC team.

Striving for Excellence in Quality

DIC Demonstration to QHSE Commitment

  • Setting clear QHSE objectives and measurable, progressive targets as part of our business performance & monitoring performance for continuous improvements.
  • Identifying, managing, and mitigating environmental risks, including the risks associated with climate change.
  • Driving HSE behaviors with a "no defects" mindset, and rewarding outstanding HSE performance and initiatives.
  • Eliminating HSE events and accidents with a strong reporting culture and effective investigation of near-misses.
  • Training its workforce on HSE standards to increase awareness of HSE risks and prevention methods.
  • Effectively communicating with stakeholders its HSE policies, standards, programs and performance.
  • Protecting the health, safety and security of its workforce at all times.
  • Meeting international, national and customer standards & requirements.
  • Protecting the environment through responsible planning and providing solutions aimed at decreasing the environmental impact of its business.
  • Ensuring HSE considerations are factored into the design, engineering, and deployment in its products and services.
  • Maintaining an updated response plan to minimize the effect of any emergency, business disruption, or crisis.
