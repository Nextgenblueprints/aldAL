Leading Innovation & Growth

Founded in 2003 as a private Company offering Heavy Equipment and Machinery support services to the Industries in Kuwait, DIC has exponentially grown and established itself as one of the most cherished multi diversified contractor across the State of Kuwait.

With dedicated and experienced Human assets of 800+ nos. and with Equipment assets exceeding 1000+ nos of Heavy Equipment & Machinery along with Innovative technological partners, we are marching towards achieving our vision of becoming a market leader and preferred partner for Integrated Energy and Infrastructure sectors services provider closer to the heart of our clients.

Our vision is to be the premier integrated energy and infrastructure partner, delivering sustainable solutions that empower communities.

Our mission is to provide world‑class services, innovative technologies, and unparalleled reliability across all business domains.
